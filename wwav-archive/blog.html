New Music Coming Soon

April 15, 2023 | Music

I'm excited to announce that I'll be releasing new music in the coming weeks. This project has been in the works for several months, and I can't wait to share it with everyone.

The new tracks explore themes of connection and introspection, with a blend of electronic and acoustic elements that I've been developing in my home studio.

Here's a sneak peek at what's to come:

  • 4 new original tracks
  • Collaboration with two guest vocalists
  • Accompanying visual elements

Stay tuned for more updates and behind-the-scenes content as we get closer to the release date.
